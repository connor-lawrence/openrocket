# Apex - Various Hobby Rocketry Projects

This is where I keep all of the `.ork` files for my various rocketry projects! Feel free to download and use some!

## Rockets

* `Hillbilly` was a small paper rocket that I created for fun to use up the A8-3 motors I had lying around... They are super easy to make and literally never flew correctly. My hypothesis is that they were so small that errors were proportionately larger compared to other vehicles... and the fins were floppy paper. That probably didn’t help either.

* `Artisan` was the step up from `Hillbilly`, made of a cardboard tube and larger to compensate for the aforementioned error. Ironically, it went even less high than the smaller rocket and failed for exactly the same reason!

* Files that start with `ARC` are for my high school’s competition vehicle for [ARC](https://rocketrychallenge.org/). Did we finish on time? Nope!

Do I care about all these shortcomings? Nope! Do I laugh at myself because it's silly? Absolutely!
